About Forest Furniture

Forest Furniture was established in 2002, to provide hand crafted bespoke furniture to both private and professional clients. Over the last decade Forest Furniture has established itself as a quality Company creating and providing beautiful, yet functional furniture designed to enhance the environment in both the home and workplace

Workshop

All work is manufactured and finished in a spacious and dedicated workshop near Alderley Edge. Your furniture will be crafted, using a combination of modern machinery, and traditional skills to create an exacting finish. An additional service includes a range of customised finishes, in either paint or lacquer, which is achieved in our dedicated spraying facility

Design

A complete service is offered to the private client; designer; architect or builder, from initial concept, through the design process, to the installation and finish of the completed commission. As a result of the knowledge and experience gained over the last decade, together with a commitment to customer service, efficient workmanship and competitive pricing, Forest Furniture is proud to say the majority of work comes from repeat business and client recommendation.

Installation

We offer a comprehensive preparation and installation service, from removal of existing furniture. Preparatory work including plastering, tiling, plumbing and electrical. We can also offer full project management for significant building alterations that may be required.
